About me

International Affairs MA Graduate

I am a graduate in International Affairs with a concentration in cities and social justice. I specialize in data visualization and design, and I am seeking to incorporate visuals and design into projects of analysis and support for the humanitarian agenda in urban contexts.

I am passionate and excited about opportunities that challenge me and the world around me. I strive to create final products that reflect the goals of the assignment in the most cohesive, high quality and visual way.

LIC Representations of Development

Social Science, Art & Mapping

To further explore the intersection of narratives on LIC I gathered representations of Long Island City (LIC) by physically going to the area, looking on the internet and by collecting developer’s proposed plans as presented by their renders, branding, propaganda, and other publications

My intention is to explore how the narratives and urban theory that support the design of the developers, ignore, disrupt or “erase” current dynamics, by creating an aspirational narrative that intends to bring them closer to Manhattan and draws them apart from Queens.

  • Research,
  • Art

Project Year: 2017

New York City Housing Authority (NYCHA)

Social Science, Social Justice & Mapping

Created a report to evaluate the performance of NYCHA’s Office for Resident Economic Empowerment & Sustainability (REES) partnership model in zone 5 (Brooklyn and Bushwick). The report focused on the effectivness the organization had on their efforts to increase residents’ income and assets through connecting them to local service providers.

Engineered maps and georeferenced data which allow NYCHA employees to explore and visualize data collected.

Produced graphs, tables, and excel sheets used for intra-organizational meetings to discuss partnership model performance.

Humanitarian Aid in South Sudan

Mapping, Data Viz & Research

Created an interactive map on the denial of medical aid in South Sudan in order to allow policy makers to better understand the conflict situation in South Sudan.

Used Mapbox, Carto, HTML, CSS and JavaScript as technical elements for map creation.

Built a georeferenced database based on over 50 reports of incidents related to humanitarian aid denial.

Generated different layers (urban, medical facilites, roads) to contextualize reported incidents.
